Detailed Description

Class for providing settings for a direct tidal acceleration model, with approach of Lainey et al. (2007, 2009, ..)

Class for providing settings for a direct tidal acceleration model, with approach of Lainey et al. (2007, 2009, ..). Using this approach does includes the effect of tides raised by/on a planetary satelltie on the orbit of the satellite by a dedicated acceleration model, instead of modifying the gravity field coefficients of the satellite/host planet/
