Detailed Description

Gas component properties data structure.

This data structure contains the molar mass and collision diameter of the most frequently observed gasses in the atmosphere.

Constructs a GasComponentProperties data structure with values obtained from the following sources: Collision diameters from: Tables of Physical & Chemical Constants Kaye & Laby Online, Kaye & Laby Online, 2016 (http://www.kayelaby.npl.co.uk/general_physics/2_2/2_2_4.html) Molar mass from: NIST,2016 (http://www.nist.gov/pml/data/images/illo_for_2014_PT_1.PNG)
