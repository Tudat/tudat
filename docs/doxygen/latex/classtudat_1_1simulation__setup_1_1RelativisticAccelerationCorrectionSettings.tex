\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Class to proivide settings for typical relativistic corrections to the dynamics of an orbiter. 

Class to proivide settings for typical relativistic corrections to the dynamics of an orbiter\+: the Schwarzschild, Lense-\/\+Thirring and de Sitter terms. An excellent introduction to these models is given in \textquotesingle{}General relativity and Space Geodesy\textquotesingle{} by L. Combrinck (2012).
